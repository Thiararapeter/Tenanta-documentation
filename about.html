Our Story

Tenanta was born from the frustration of managing properties with outdated tools and fragmented systems. Our founder, a property manager himself, experienced firsthand the challenges of tracking tenants, managing bills, and generating reports across multiple properties.

In 2023, we set out to create a comprehensive solution that would address these pain points. Built with modern technology and designed with user experience in mind, Tenanta has grown to serve property managers across multiple countries.

Today, we continue to innovate and expand our platform, always keeping our users' needs at the center of everything we do.
