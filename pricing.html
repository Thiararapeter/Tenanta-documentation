Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

Is there a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial for all plans. No credit card required to start.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.

Yes! We use enterprise-grade security with data encryption, regular backups, and GDPR compliance.

Yes! We offer special pricing for non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team for details.

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. No cancellation fees or long-term contracts.
