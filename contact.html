Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I get started?

You can start using Tenanta immediately with our 14-day free trial. No credit card required, and you can have your first property set up in under 10 minutes.

Do you offer training and onboarding?

Yes! We provide comprehensive onboarding for all new users, including video tutorials, documentation, and personalized training sessions for Enterprise customers.

Can I import my existing data?

Absolutely! We support data import from Excel, CSV files, and most popular property management systems. Our team can help with the migration process.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer email support for all users, priority support for Professional plans, and dedicated support with phone access for Enterprise customers.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use enterprise-grade security with data encryption, regular backups, and compliance with GDPR and other international data protection standards.

Do you offer custom integrations?

Yes! Enterprise customers have access to our API for custom integrations. We also offer professional services to help build custom solutions.
